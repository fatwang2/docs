## Setting up

Search1API is a versatile search aggregation service that enables you to perform searches across Google, Bing, and DuckDuckGo, also, you can get clear content from the url. Accessible via a POST request, this API offers a cost-effective solution at just $0.99 USD for 1,000 queries per month. To acquire your API key, simply visit https://search2ai.online/api.
